Sticky Password is a password manager designed to securely store passwords and other sensitive information. It uses encryption to protect your data and allows you to access your password vault across supported devices.

Although Sticky Password is generally reliable, occasional login issues may occur after software updates, browser changes, network interruptions, or incorrect account information. Understanding the possible causes makes troubleshooting much easier.

Why Is Sticky Password Login Not Working?
-----------------------------------------

There are several reasons why you may be unable to sign in.

Common causes include:

* Incorrect email address
* Incorrect Master Password
* Internet connectivity problems
* Browser extension conflicts
* Outdated Sticky Password software
* Corrupted browser cache or cookies
* Firewall or VPN restrictions
* Device synchronization problems
* Security verification issues
* Temporary server or synchronization interruptions

Check Your Login Credentials
----------------------------

Before trying advanced troubleshooting, verify your account information.

Make sure:

* The email address belongs to your Sticky Password account.
* The Master Password is entered correctly.
* Caps Lock is turned off.
* The correct keyboard layout is selected.
* There are no extra spaces before or after the password.

Even a small typing error can prevent successful authentication.

Verify Your Internet Connection
-------------------------------

Sticky Password may require a stable internet connection for account authentication and synchronization.

Try the following:

* Restart your modem or router.
* Connect to another Wi-Fi network.
* Test another internet connection.
* Disable VPN or proxy services temporarily.
* Confirm that other websites are loading normally.

Update Sticky Password
----------------------

Running an outdated version of the application can sometimes lead to login or synchronization problems.

Check for updates if you use:

* Windows
* macOS
* Android
* iPhone
* iPad

After installing updates:

#. Restart the application.
#. Restart your device.
#. Try logging in again.

Troubleshoot Browser Extension Problems
---------------------------------------

If the problem only occurs in your browser, the extension may require attention.

Try these steps:

#. Close and reopen your browser.
#. Update the Sticky Password extension.
#. Clear browser cache and cookies.
#. Disable other extensions that may conflict.
#. Remove and reinstall the Sticky Password extension.

Supported browsers may include Chrome, Edge, Firefox, and other compatible browsers depending on the available extension.

Clear Browser Cache and Cookies
-------------------------------

Old browser data can interfere with login sessions.

To clear cached information:

#. Open browser settings.
#. Remove cached files and cookies.
#. Restart the browser.
#. Attempt to sign in again.

This can often resolve authentication or autofill issues.

Forgot Your Master Password?
----------------------------

Your Master Password is required to unlock your encrypted password vault.

If you cannot remember it:

* Check your secure password records.
* Review available recovery options.
* Follow Sticky Password's recovery instructions if recovery has been configured.

Because password managers are designed with strong encryption, your Master Password cannot normally be retrieved by the provider.

Check Device Date and Time
--------------------------

Incorrect date or time settings may interfere with secure authentication.

Verify that your device:

* Displays the correct date.
* Uses the correct time zone.
* Synchronizes automatically with a trusted time service.

Restart Your Device
-------------------

Temporary software problems can sometimes be fixed by restarting your device.

Restart your:

* Computer
* Smartphone
* Tablet

After restarting, open Sticky Password and try signing in again.

Mobile Login Issues
-------------------

If the issue occurs on Android or iPhone:

* Install the latest version of Sticky Password.
* Restart the device.
* Check your internet connection.
* Verify biometric authentication settings.
* Sign in again using your Master Password if necessary.

Desktop Login Issues
--------------------

If you cannot sign in through the desktop application:

* Restart Sticky Password.
* Install available software updates.
* Check firewall or antivirus settings.
* Restart your computer.
* Try signing in again.

Sticky Password Security Features

Sticky Password includes several security features to help protect your information.

These include:

* Strong encryption

* Secure synchronization

Tips to Prevent Future Login Problems
-------------------------------------

Following good security practices can reduce future login issues.

Recommendations include:

* Create a strong and unique Master Password.
* Keep Sticky Password updated.
* Use trusted internet connections.
* Avoid signing in on public computers.
* Regularly back up important account information if supported.
* Store recovery information securely.
* Review your saved credentials periodically.

When Should You Contact Support?
--------------------------------

Consider contacting Sticky Password support if:

* Login continues to fail after completing all troubleshooting steps.
* The application repeatedly crashes during sign-in.
* Synchronization never completes.
* You believe your account has been compromised.
* You continue receiving unexpected authentication errors.

Frequently Asked Questions
--------------------------

Why is Sticky Password login not working?
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

Login problems are commonly caused by incorrect credentials, outdated software, browser extension conflicts, network connectivity issues, or authentication problems.

Will reinstalling Sticky Password delete my passwords?
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

Reinstalling the application generally does not remove information stored in your account, but locally cached data or offline changes may need to sync again after you sign in.

Can browser cache cause Sticky Password login issues?
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

Yes. Corrupted or outdated browser cache and cookies can sometimes interfere with authentication or browser extension functionality.

Can antivirus software block Sticky Password?
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

In some cases, firewall or antivirus settings may interfere with network communication or application behavior. Review your security software settings if you suspect this is the cause.

How can I avoid future login problems?
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

Keep Sticky Password updated, use a strong Master Password, maintain a stable internet connection, store recovery information securely, and regularly review your account security settings.
